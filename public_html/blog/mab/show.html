Multi-Armed Bandits

Optimal decision making in the face of uncertainty.

There are four slot machines to choose from V0, V1, V2 and V3. They each have a chance of winning [0.1, 0.3, 0.7, 0.9]. We have 1000 oppurtunities to choose a slot machines (horizon). We start with $1000. It costs $1 to use a slot machine. If we win, we get two dollars, if we loose, we loosed the dollar.
